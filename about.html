Viazi

Our Story


Viazi LLC was created by Isaac Rudich and Avi Rudich in 2017. Isaac graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in 2017 with two degrees: Production Technology & Management (College of Fine Arts) and Finance (Tepper School of Business). Avi expects to graduate from Carnegie Mellon University in 2020 with a degree in Electrical Engineering from the College of Engineering


Timeline

  • March 2017

    Isaac’s undergraduate advisor tells him that he spends most of his time working on next semester’s schedule. The advisor has looked for software to solve this problem, but none can handle the complex scenarios that arise when space and time are both heavily constrained.

  • April 2017

    Isaac and Avi begin work on an algorithm and platform to solve the scheduling problem.

  • September 2017

    Isaac and Avi have created an algorithm that can solve the problem in hours, instead of the weeks that other modern algorithms take. Viazi LLC is officially incorporated.

  • December 2017

    Viazi demonstrates an installable software that takes in necessary constraints and creates a schedule. Our first client expresses immense interest in using the software, but requests a website be made for long term use.

  • July 2018

    A breakthrough for our algorithm reduces its run time to less than a minute on most data sets.

  • August 2018

    The website version of Viazi solver goes live.
